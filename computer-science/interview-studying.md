### Modular Arithmetic
We denote by $x \bmod m$ the remainder when $x$ is divided by $m$. For example, $17 \bmod 5=2$, because $17=3 \cdot 5+2$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
(a+b) \bmod m & =(a \bmod m+b \bmod m) \bmod m \\
(a-b) \bmod m & =(a \bmod m-b \bmod m) \bmod m \\
(a \cdot b) \bmod m & =(a \bmod m \cdot b \bmod m) \bmod m
\end{aligned}
$$
