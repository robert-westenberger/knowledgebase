# Telescoping Sum
Doesn't have a set form. It is any series where nearly every term cancels with a preceding or following term. 

For instance, $1^{2}-2^{2}+3^{2}-4^{2}+\ldots+(-1)^{k-1} k^{2}$ = $\sum_{k=1}^{n}(-1)^{k-1} k^{2}$ is telescoping.
