# Trigonometry


$$
\sin \Theta = \frac{opposite}{hypotenuse} 
\\[3ex]
\cos \Theta = \frac{hypotenuse}{adjacent}
\\[3ex]
\tan \Theta = \frac{\sin \Theta}{\cos \Theta} = \frac{opposite / hypotenuse}{adjacent/hypotenuse} = \frac{opposite}{adjacent} 
$$
