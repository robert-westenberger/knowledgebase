## How to Prove $a \in A$

* To show $\mathbf{a} \in\{\mathbf{x}: \mathbf{P}(\mathbf{x})\}$ 	
	* show that $P(a)$ is true.

* To show $\mathbf{a} \in\{\mathbf{x} \in \mathbf{S}: \mathbf{P}(\mathbf{x})\}$
	* Verify that $a \in S$
  * Show that $P(a)$ is true.
