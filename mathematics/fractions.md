# Fractions
## Simplifying Fractions
Simplify 
$$\frac{1}{3x}-\frac{1}{4x^2}$$
1) Put fractions over a common denominator (in this case, $12x^2$)
2) Combine into single fraction 
$$\frac{4x-3}{12x^2}$$
