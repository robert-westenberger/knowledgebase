# Product Rule
$\log _{a}(x y)=\log _{a}(x)+\log _{a}(y)$
The log of a product is equal to the sum of the logs of its factors.

# Change of Base Rule
$\log _{a} b=\frac{\log _{c} b}{\log _{c} a}$
