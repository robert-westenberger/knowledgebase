# Exponential Functions
Let $n$ be a positive integer, and let $b$ be a fixed positive real number. The function $f_{b}(n)=b^{n}$ is defined by
$$
f_{b}(n)=b^{n}=b \cdot b \cdot b \cdot \cdots \cdot b
$$
